About PakVista

Pakistan is a land of extraordinary contrasts — from the towering peaks of the Karakoram and Himalayas in the north to the sun-drenched shores of the Arabian Sea in the south. Its cities pulse with centuries of Mughal history, Sufi tradition, and modern energy, while its valleys offer some of the most dramatic landscapes on Earth.

PakVista is your curated guide to five of Pakistan's must-visit destinations. Whether you are drawn to ancient monuments, glacier-fed lakes, bustling bazaars, or serene mountain meadows, Pakistan has something extraordinary waiting for you. Browse our destination pages to plan your next adventure.

Why Visit Pakistan?

Pakistan is home to five of the world's fourteen peaks above 8,000 metres, thousands of kilometres of ancient trade routes, Mughal architecture rivalling that of any dynasty in history, and a hospitality tradition that has made it one of the most warmly welcoming nations for international travellers. In recent years, tourism has surged as adventurers and culture enthusiasts alike discover what Pakistanis have always known — that this country is one of the world's best-kept secrets.

From the walled city of Lahore to the glacial valleys of Gilgit-Baltistan, from the mangroves of the Indus Delta to the tombs of Multan's saints, every corner of Pakistan offers a new story. PakVista is here to help you find yours.
